pizza

Pizza (Italian: ['pittsa], Neapolitan: [ 'pittse]) is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other Ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms. onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which IS then baked at a high temperature. tradationally In a wood fire oven. [1] A small pizza Is sometimes called a pizzetta. A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo.
